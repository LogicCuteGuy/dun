# Minor Arcana (56 cards)

The 56 "daily" cards. They mark moods, situations, choices — not the major themes of life.

Two layers combine: **suit** (what *area*) + **rank** (what *stage*).

---

## Suits

| Suit | Element | Domain |
|---|---|---|
| **Wands** | Fire | energy, drive, willpower, creativity, passion |
| **Cups** | Water | emotion, feelings, intuition, relationships, the heart |
| **Swords** | Air | logic, ideas, intellect, communication, conflict |
| **Pentacles** | Earth | nature, body, material world, stability, security |

### Reversed suit cues
- Wands reversed: blocked energy, burnout, recklessness
- Cups reversed: emotional loss, blocked creativity, emptiness
- Swords reversed: confusion, brutality, chaos
- Pentacles reversed: lost opportunity, disorganized, overwhelmed

---

## Ranks

### Numbered cards (Ace – 10)

The number tells you **where in the cycle** the suit's energy sits.

| Rank | Stage |
|---|---|
| **Ace** | seed — pure potential, a fresh touch, new feelings |
| **2** | duality — choice, polarity, balance, partnership |
| **3** | expansion — first growth, early synthesis, community |
| **4** | foundation — stability, structure, holding, contemplation |
| **5** | friction — conflict, loss, instability, competition |
| **6** | flow — communication, harmony, victory, transition |
| **7** | assessment — defense, introspection, stance, perseverance |
| **8** | momentum — mastery in motion, near-completion, rapid action |
| **9** | culmination — near the end of the cycle, resilience |
| **10** | completion — full cycle, overflow, ending, accomplishment |

#### Special cases

- **3 of Cups** (upright) = friendship, community, happiness, celebration — the *one* card where 3 shifts feel festive rather than merely expansive.
- **10 of Swords** (upright) = failure, collapse, defeat, rock bottom — the *one* card where 10 lands as defeat, not just fullness.
- **10 of Cups** (upright) = inner happiness, fulfillment, dreams coming true — the *one* card where 10 is pure joy.

### Court cards (Page, Knight, Queen, King)

The court cards are *people* (or aspects of self), not events.

| Card | Energy |
|---|---|
| **Page** | youth of the suit — message, beginner, spark, curiosity |
| **Knight** | action of the suit — pursuit, movement, drive, adventure |
| **Queen** | mastery-in-self — receptive, mature, internal, compassion |
| **King** | mastery-in-world — directive, embodied, external, leadership |

When a court card appears, ask: *who in the user's life is this?* or *which part of themselves is being asked to step into this role?*

## Quick draw recipe

When a Minor card lands:

1. **Suit** → *what area* (energy / feeling / thinking / matter)
2. **Number** → *what stage* (seed / choice / growth / hold / friction / flow / assess / move / near-end / end)
3. **Reversed or upright** → *internalized or externalized*

Combine in plain language. Example:

> **5 of Cups reversed** — feeling-side, friction stage, internalized. *Something painful turning soft; the loss is still there but the user is starting to look at what remains.*

That's enough. Don't over-elaborate. The Major Arcana tell the story; Minor cards *populate* it.
