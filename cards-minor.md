# Minor Arcana (56 cards)

The 56 "daily" cards. They mark moods, situations, choices — not the major themes of life.

Two layers combine: **suit** (what *area*) + **rank** (what *stage*).

---

## Suits

| Suit | Element | Domain |
|---|---|---|
| **Wands** | Fire | energy, action, creativity, work, will |
| **Cups** | Water | emotion, relationships, intuition, the heart |
| **Swords** | Air | thought, conflict, truth, intellect |
| **Pentacles** | Earth | body, money, health, material world |

### Reversed suit cues
- Wands reversed: blocked energy, burnout
- Cups reversed: emotional avoidance, overflow
- Swords reversed: confusion, avoidance of truth
- Pentacles reversed: scarcity thinking, lost grip on body/health

---

## Ranks

### Numbered cards (Ace – 10)

The number tells you **where in the cycle** the suit's energy sits.

| Rank | Stage |
|---|---|
| **Ace** | seed — pure potential, a fresh touch |
| **2** | duality — choice, polarity, balance needed |
| **3** | expansion — first growth, early synthesis |
| **4** | foundation — stability, structure, holding |
| **5** | friction — conflict, loss, instability |
| **6** | flow — communication, harmony, victory |
| **7** | assessment — defense, introspection, stance |
| **8** | momentum — mastery in motion, near-completion |
| **9** | culmination — near the end of the cycle |
| **10** | completion — full cycle, overflow, ending |

#### Two special cases

- **3 of Cups** (upright) = celebration, friendship, joy among kin — the *one* card where 3 shifts feel festive rather than merely expansive.
- **10 of Swords** (upright) = rock bottom, a brutal ending — the *one* card where 10 lands as defeat, not just fullness.

### Court cards (Page, Knight, Queen, King)

The court cards are *people* (or aspects of self), not events.

| Card | Energy |
|---|---|
| **Page** | youth of the suit — message, beginner, spark |
| **Knight** | action of the suit — pursuit, movement, drive |
| **Queen** | mastery-in-self — receptive, mature, internal |
| **King** | mastery-in-world — directive, embodied, external |

When a court card appears, ask: *who in the user's life is this?* or *which part of themselves is being asked to step into this role?*

---

## Quick draw recipe

When a Minor card lands:

1. **Suit** → *what area* (energy / feeling / thinking / matter)
2. **Number** → *what stage* (seed / choice / growth / hold / friction / flow / assess / move / near-end / end)
3. **Reversed or upright** → *internalized or externalized*

Combine in plain language. Example:

> **5 of Cups reversed** — feeling-side, friction stage, internalized. *Something painful turning soft; the loss is still there but the user is starting to look at what remains.*

That's enough. Don't over-elaborate. The Major Arcana tell the story; Minor cards *populate* it.
